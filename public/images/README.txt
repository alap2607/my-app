RECIPE IMAGES - INSTRUCTIONS
============================

Add your recipe photos to this directory with the following names:

Required Images:
----------------
1. paneer-butter-masala.jpg  - Creamy Paneer Butter Masala photo
2. tandoori-chicken.jpg       - Tandoori Chicken photo
3. dal-tadka.jpg             - Dal Tadka photo
4. chicken-tacos.jpg         - Spicy Chicken Tacos photo

Recommended Image Specifications:
---------------------------------
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Minimum Size: 600px x 400px
- Aspect Ratio: 3:2 (landscape orientation works best)
- File Size: Keep under 500KB for faster loading

Tips for Best Results:
---------------------
- Use high-quality, well-lit food photography
- Ensure the dish is the main focus of the image
- Consider using a neutral background to make the food stand out
- Images will be automatically cropped to fit the card layout

Once you add the images, they will automatically appear on your website!
